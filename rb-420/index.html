LOCAL-FIRST PROJECT CONTROL

Project & Documentation

01

Zero install

Open index.html directly. No server, Node, npm, build step, framework, CDN, telemetry, cookies or network requests.

02

Original synthesis

Acid voices and drum sounds are synthesized in the browser. The bundle contains no Roland, ReBirth, Impulse Tracker or commercial sample assets.

03

Two recorder contracts

Deterministic Render freezes a recipe and uses seeded PCM synthesis. Live Capture records the actual browser performance and is intentionally take-specific.

04

Tracker bridge

Rendered, recorded and imported loops become tracker instruments. Sequence them in the 8-channel Impulse-style grid, then render another loop.

05

Open formats

Projects and patterns are JSON; audio is PCM16 WAV. Native RB-420 files do not masquerade as proprietary .rbs or .it files.

06

Creative utilities

Seeded mutation, Euclidean rhythm generation, waveform scopes, sample import, project export, keyboard entry and the guitar/synth Sound Lab are included.
